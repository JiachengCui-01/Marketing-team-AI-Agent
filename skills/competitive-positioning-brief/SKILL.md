# Competitive Positioning Brief

Use this skill when the user needs a competitor comparison, differentiation narrative, battlecard, or positioning memo.

## What This Skill Produces

- Competitive landscape summary
- Differentiation pillars and proof points
- Competitor-by-competitor comparison
- Sales and marketing messaging guidance
- Risks, caveats, and research gaps

## Workflow

1. Clarify the product, competitors, target segment, and decision context.
2. Separate factual evidence from assumptions or claims needing verification.
3. Compare competitors by customer pain, feature fit, pricing signal, proof, and positioning.
4. Turn differences into concise messaging, objection handling, and content angles.

## Output Rules

- Do not invent competitor facts when sources or user materials are missing.
- Mark unverifiable claims as assumptions.
- Prefer decision-useful comparisons over broad market summaries.
- Include recommended source documents or files to collect next.
