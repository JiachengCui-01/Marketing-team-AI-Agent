# Product Launch Campaign

Use this skill when the user needs an integrated launch plan for a new product, feature, service, or campaign.

## What This Skill Produces

- Launch positioning and audience segmentation
- Channel plan for owned, paid, and social channels
- Campaign timeline with pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases
- Core messages, CTA options, and deliverable checklist

## Workflow

1. Clarify the product, audience, goal, market, timeline, and available channels.
2. Identify the primary promise, proof points, objections, and conversion path.
3. Build a phased launch plan with owners, content needs, and measurement points.
4. Provide copy-ready messaging blocks and next actions.

## Output Rules

- Keep recommendations practical for a marketing team to execute.
- Separate assumptions from confirmed user inputs.
- Include KPI suggestions and measurement cadence.
- When information is missing, ask for the smallest set of high-impact clarifications.
