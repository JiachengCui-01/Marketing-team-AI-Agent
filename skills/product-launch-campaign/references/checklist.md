# Launch Checklist

- Product name and one-sentence description
- Target customer segment and buying trigger
- Launch objective: awareness, leads, trial, purchase, retention, or expansion
- Core offer and CTA
- Primary channels
- Timeline and budget constraints
- Proof assets: demo, testimonial, case study, benchmark, customer quote, screenshot
- Required deliverables: landing page, email, social posts, ads, sales enablement, webinar, press note
